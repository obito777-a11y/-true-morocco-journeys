Ouarzazate

Ouarzazate Kasbah Trail

2 Days / 1 Night 2–10 Guests 4.7 (106 reviews)

Where ancient mud and Hollywood meet

Ouarzazate sits at the gateway to the Sahara — a dusty, sun-baked town that has become Morocco's film industry hub. Game of Thrones, Gladiator, Lawrence of Arabia — all filmed here or nearby. But the real draw is Ait Benhaddou, a UNESCO-listed mudbrick kasbah that has been continuously inhabited for over a thousand years.

Two days gives you time for both the kasbah and the Draa Valley beyond it.

Highlights

  • Ait Benhaddou UNESCO kasbah guided visit
  • CLA Studios — Morocco's largest film studio
  • Draa Valley and palm oasis drive
  • Tifoultoute kasbah and Ouarzazate town
  • Local kasbahs still inhabited by Berber families
  • Sunset from the kasbah ridge
1Day

Marrakesh → Tizi n'Tichka → Ait Benhaddou

Over the dramatic Tizi n'Tichka pass (2,260m) and down into the pre-Sahara. Guided visit to Ait Benhaddou, including the climb to the ridge for panoramic views. Overnight in Ouarzazate.

2Day

Film Studios + Draa Valley + Return

Morning visit to the CLA Studios. Then east along the Draa Valley through palm oases and smaller kasbahs, before the return drive to Marrakesh.

Included

  • 1 night in Ouarzazate
  • Breakfast included
  • Expert guide for all sites
  • 4x4 transport throughout
  • Ait Benhaddou entrance fee
  • Studio visit (exterior + select sets)

Not Included

  • International flights
  • Lunches and dinners (day 1 dinner on request)
  • Personal shopping
  • Travel insurance
