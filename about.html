About True Morocco Explorer

Founded by Moroccan-born guides in 2012, built on the idea that the best trips don't follow a script.

Our Story

Started by guides, not a booking platform

True Morocco Explorer began with three friends who'd spent years driving tourists through the same five photo stops and watching them miss everything in between. In 2012 we set out to build trips that actually reflected the country we grew up in — the unmarked tea houses, the back roads through the Atlas, the families who'll cook you dinner if you show up with the right introduction.

More than a decade later, we're still a small team. Every itinerary on this site has been driven, walked, or slept on by someone who works here — we don't sell routes we haven't done ourselves.
