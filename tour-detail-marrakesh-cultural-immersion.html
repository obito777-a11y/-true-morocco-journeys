Marrakesh

Marrakesh Cultural Immersion

4 Days / 3 Nights 2–10 Guests 4.9 (214 reviews)

The city that never fully reveals itself

Four days is the minimum you need to peel back Marrakesh's many layers. The first day disorientation is part of the experience — the second day you start to read the medina's logic. By day four you'll have a favourite café, a carpet-maker friend, and a shortcut through the souks only locals know.

Your guide lives here. This isn't a highlights tour — it's an introduction to a city through the eyes of someone who grew up in it.

Highlights

  • Guided medina walk through the souks
  • Majorelle Garden & Yves Saint Laurent Museum
  • Cooking class with a local family
  • Evening at Jemaa el-Fnaa square
  • Riad accommodation in the heart of the medina
  • Hidden hammam experience
1Day

Arrival + Medina Orientation

Settle into your riad, then a gentle afternoon walk to orient yourself around the medina. Evening at Jemaa el-Fnaa to watch the square come alive with storytellers and musicians.

2Day

Souks, Artisans & Palaces

Full-day guided walk through the souks — leather, spices, textiles. Visit the Bahia Palace and the Saadian Tombs. Lunch at a rooftop restaurant with medina views.

3Day

Cooking Class + Majorelle Garden

Morning cooking class with a local family — market shopping included. Afternoon at the Majorelle Garden and the YSL Museum. Free evening to explore independently.

4Day

Day Trip to the Atlas Foothills

Short drive to the Ourika Valley in the High Atlas — Berber villages, a waterfall, and lunch by the river. Back to Marrakesh by late afternoon for departure or an extra night.

Included

  • 3 nights in a central medina riad
  • Daily breakfasts + 2 dinners
  • Expert local guide for all tours
  • Cooking class & market visit
  • Entrance fees for all sites
  • Airport transfers

Not Included

  • International flights
  • Lunches (except cooking class day)
  • Personal shopping
  • Travel insurance
