Casablanca

Casablanca City Highlights

1 Day 2–12 Guests 4.5 (112 reviews)

Morocco's modern side — and one extraordinary mosque

Most travellers pass through Casablanca on the way somewhere else. That's understandable — it's not a typical Moroccan medina city. But the Hassan II Mosque is one of the most extraordinary buildings in the world, and the Art Deco centre is genuinely beautiful if you know where to look.

This guided day gives you both, plus the corniche and a proper Casablancais lunch.

Highlights

  • Hassan II Mosque interior guided visit
  • Art Deco boulevards walking tour
  • Old Medina and Habous quarter
  • Ain Diab corniche and Atlantic seafront
  • Traditional lunch in the Habous
  • Mohamed V Square and central architecture
1Day

Hassan II Mosque + Art Deco Centre

Begin at the Hassan II Mosque — the guided interior tour takes about 45 minutes and is genuinely stunning. Then walk through the Art Deco centre around Boulevard Mohamed V.

2Day

Habous + Lunch + Corniche

Lunch in the Habous quarter, the French-built 'new medina'. Afternoon at the Ain Diab corniche — coffee with an Atlantic view before departure.

Included

  • Local expert guide
  • Hassan II Mosque entrance and guided tour
  • Transport between sites
  • Lunch in the Habous quarter

Not Included

  • International flights or train tickets
  • Personal shopping
  • Drinks beyond lunch
  • Travel insurance
