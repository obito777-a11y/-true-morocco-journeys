High Atlas Mountains

Atlas Mountains Trekking

6 Days / 5 Nights 2–8 Guests 4.9 (98 reviews)

Slow travel through Morocco's rooftop

An hour from Marrakesh, the High Atlas feels like a completely different country. Terraced barley fields, walnut groves, and mudbrick villages clinging to hillsides — this is Berber Morocco, largely unchanged for centuries.

This six-day trek takes you through Imlil, up toward the Toubkal base camp, and through three valleys most visitors never reach. Mule support carries your bags; you carry only a daypack.

Highlights

  • Trek through Imlil and the Ait Mizane valley
  • Toubkal base camp overnight (3,207m)
  • Meals and nights in Berber family guesthouses
  • Mule support for luggage throughout
  • Panoramic views of North Africa's highest peak
  • Local Berber guide, born in the Atlas
1Day

Marrakesh → Imlil → First Village

Drive to Imlil (1,740m), meet your guide and mules, then a 3-hour introductory walk to the first village guesthouse. Dinner with the host family.

2Day

Ait Mizane Valley Trek

Full trekking day through the valley, passing walnut groves and terraced fields. Lunch by a river, overnight in a remote Berber village.

3Day

Ascent to Toubkal Base Camp

The most demanding day — a steady 5-hour climb to the Toubkal refuge at 3,207m. Spectacular sunset views over the Atlas range.

4Day

Base Camp → Lac d'Ifni

An alternative descent route via the beautiful Lac d'Ifni, a high-altitude glacial lake. Overnight in tents beside the lake.

5Day

Descent through the Ouaneskra Valley

A long but rewarding descent through a less-visited valley, finishing at a roadside guesthouse with a hot shower and a proper dinner.

6Day

Return to Marrakesh

Morning walk out to the trailhead, drive back to Marrakesh. Arrive by early afternoon.

Included

  • 5 nights' accommodation (guesthouses + 1 night camping)
  • All meals on trek (full board)
  • Certified local Berber guide
  • Mule support for luggage
  • National park entrance fees
  • Transport Marrakesh–Imlil–Marrakesh

Not Included

  • International flights
  • Personal trekking gear (list provided on booking)
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips for guide and muleteers
