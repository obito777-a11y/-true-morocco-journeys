Chefchaouen

Chefchaouen Blue City Tour

2 Days / 1 Night 2–10 Guests 4.7 (131 reviews)

Every wall is painted. Every corner is a photograph.

Chefchaouen sits in a fold of the Rif Mountains, three hours from Fes or Tangier. The entire medina is painted in shades of blue — cobalt, powder, indigo — a tradition that began in the 1930s with Jewish refugees and never stopped.

Two days is the right amount of time: enough to find your own corners, slow down, and not feel like you're ticking a box.

Highlights

  • Guided walk through the blue medina
  • Ras el-Ma waterfall and Spanish mosque viewpoint
  • Rif Mountains short hike above the city
  • Local market visit and argan oil cooperative
  • Overnight in a charming blue-tiled riad
  • Free afternoon to wander independently
1Day

Arrival + Medina Walk

Arrive by mid-morning, check into your riad, then a 2-hour guided walk through the medina's most photogenic corners. Visit the central Plaza Uta el-Hammam and the kasbah museum. Evening free to explore.

2Day

Mountains + Market + Departure

Morning hike above the city to the Spanish mosque for panoramic views. Visit the local market, then the argan oil cooperative just outside town. Afternoon departure.

Included

  • 1 night in a blue medina riad
  • Breakfast included
  • Expert local guide for both days
  • Entrance to kasbah museum
  • Transport from/to Fes or Tangier (on request)

Not Included

  • International flights
  • Lunches and dinners
  • Personal shopping
  • Travel insurance
