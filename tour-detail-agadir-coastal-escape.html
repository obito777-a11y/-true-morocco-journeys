Agadir

Agadir Coastal Escape

3 Days / 2 Nights 2–12 Guests 4.6 (89 reviews)

Long beaches and a slower pace

Rebuilt entirely after the devastating 1960 earthquake, Agadir is Morocco's most relaxed city — wide boulevards, a 10km beach, and a restaurant scene that leans heavily on Atlantic seafood.

This three-day escape combines beach time with a day trip to the Souss Valley and the argan forest — a UNESCO biosphere reserve where wild goats famously climb the trees to eat the fruit.

Highlights

  • Agadir beach and Atlantic promenade
  • Souss-Massa National Park excursion
  • Argan forest and goats-in-trees visit
  • Agadir Oufella kasbah ruins at sunset
  • Fresh seafood lunch at the fishing port
  • Surf lesson option (beginner-friendly)
1Day

Arrival + Beach + Port

Arrive and settle in. Afternoon walk along the beach promenade, then a seafood lunch at the fishing port. Evening stroll through the marina.

2Day

Souss Valley + Argan Forest

Full-day excursion into the Souss Valley — the argan forest, an argan oil women's cooperative, and the Souss-Massa National Park with its flamingo colony.

3Day

Kasbah Ruins + Departure

Morning visit to the Agadir Oufella ruins on the hill above the city — panoramic views of the bay. Return to your hotel and afternoon departure.

Included

  • 2 nights in a beach hotel
  • Daily breakfasts
  • Local guide for all excursions
  • Transport for Souss Valley day trip
  • Argan cooperative visit

Not Included

  • International flights
  • Lunches and dinners (except day 1 port lunch)
  • Surf lessons (available on request, extra cost)
  • Travel insurance
