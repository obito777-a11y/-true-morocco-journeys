Erg Chigaga, Sahara

Luxury Desert Glamping

2 Days / 1 Night 2–8 Guests 4.9 (74 reviews)

The desert, without roughing it

Erg Chigaga is Morocco's quieter dune field — further south than Erg Chebbi, harder to reach, and almost entirely empty. The camp here has en-suite tents with proper beds, a private chef, and solar power. It's as comfortable as a good hotel, with an infinite sky above it.

Getting there requires a 4x4 across a 50km desert piste — that journey is part of the experience.

Highlights

  • En-suite desert tent with real beds
  • Private chef and candlelit dinners
  • 4x4 access across 50km desert piste
  • Camel sunset ride to the dune crest
  • Stargazing session with a guide
  • Complete digital detox — no Wi-Fi, by design
1Day

M'Hamid → Erg Chigaga by 4x4

Depart M'Hamid by 4x4 across the desert piste — 2.5 hours with stops at oases and fossils along the way. Arrive at camp by early afternoon. Sunset camel ride. Candlelit dinner under the stars.

2Day

Sunrise + Breakfast + Return

Wake before sunrise to climb the dunes. Breakfast served in your tent. Return to M'Hamid by 4x4, with a stop at a nomad camp along the way.

Included

  • 1 night en-suite desert tent
  • All meals (dinner + breakfast)
  • 4x4 transport to/from M'Hamid
  • Sunset camel ride
  • Stargazing session
  • Bottled water throughout

Not Included

  • Transport to/from M'Hamid (Marrakesh transfer available on request)
  • Alcoholic drinks
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips for camp staff
